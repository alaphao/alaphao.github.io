Context

42412 - CBA ILT Phase 1] - There is an idea at ECG that by consolidating the whole of CBA program's resources pool data into centralized system will further improve our understanding of their capabilities, thus allowing our VMO to better serve them, via there Planning and Execution work.

Also, the need to have this information recorded in a fit for purpose system that can supply, in real-time, the analytics required to answer the basic question "who's available to do what work", has become more pressing as ECG takes on the work management of Multiple Bidders for which there is an added layer of complexity in assessing a resource's ability to do work, due to confidentiality agreements between the bank and its clients.

Furthermore, as Ensemble grows and take on new customers, it is of value to us as an organisation to understand how we can better use Maestro (Integrated with Concerto) as a resource management tool, in different industries/environments, so we can continue to refine our MaestroUX software development efforts.

Purpose

To aid the VMO's effort to improve resource management practices at CBA, starting with the Multiple Bidders body of work, by methodically defining and populating the various elements of their resource pool/hierarchy within MaestroUX. This information can thereafter be used in real-time to evaluate said resource pool's capacity to do work. and also to define the processes by which that information will be maintained
